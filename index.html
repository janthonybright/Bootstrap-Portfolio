About Me

My name is Joey Bright, I am a new fullstack developer from the University of Utah fullstack program. I have worked in the film industry for the past 12 years as a Location Manager and Location Scout. This work involved high levels of stress and very strict time frames, which has taught me strong interpersonal skills along with working within a team.

A few of my hobbies outside of work are video games, rock climbing, and skateboarding. I like to try new things and eat new foods, especially if they are spicy. My loving, supportive wife and I have been married for 2 years. We live in North Salt Lake with our two dogs Finn, and Peanut, who love adventurous hikes. I am currently working on my coding skills in the boot camp and any freelance film projects that fit inbetween.
